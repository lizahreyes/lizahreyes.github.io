Welcome to the recipe page!

Diane is my younger sister and she has greatly inspired my involvement in the field of food and nutrition. At a very young age, we found out that Diane had a variety of food allergies including: dairy products, seafood, nuts, and eggs. Although food allergies have become a more well-known thing nowadays, they were not so recognized ~20 years ago and at that time my family and I had no idea what challenges were ahead of us.

These recipes are near-and-dear to my heart because they are the ones (of many, many recipes) that Diane approves of!
