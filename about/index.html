Hi everyone, my name is Lizah Reyes and I am super thankful that you took the time to drop by my website!

I am currently in my final year of the Nutrition and Food program at Ryerson University, and will be applying for a post-graduate internship for the following year. Before I started at Ryerson, I had completed my undergraduate studies at the University of Toronto. From there, my interest in nutrition grew and drove my further interest in wanting to become a Registered Dietitian.

I have spent the last few years volunteering and working in various positions in the community, in hospitals, and in research, and from these experiences I have learned that this profession is where I truly belong.
